Travel Story

Exploring Seoul's Hidden Gems

“Lose yourself in the timeless alleys of Seoul, where tradition whispers through ancient palaces and vibrant markets pulse with life. Every corner reveals a story waiting to be discovered!”

  • Wander through hidden hanok villages and experience authentic Korean street food at bustling markets.
  • Marvel at Seoul’s centuries-old royal palaces where tradition meets modern life — every turn promises a new discovery!
  • Explore the quiet serenity of Bukchon Hanok Village, where traditional rooftops overlook a vibrant city skyline.
  • Venture into Ikseon-dong’s charming alleyways filled with artisan cafés and craft boutiques.
  • Savor sizzling tteokbokki, hotteok, and fresh seafood skewers at lively night markets like Gwangjang and Namdaemun.
  • Stand in awe before Gyeongbokgung Palace, where guards in colorful hanbok reenact royal ceremonies amidst grand courtyards and stone pathways.
  • Discover hidden tea houses tucked behind wooden gates and listen to street musicians serenading busy plazas.
  • Seoul invites you to slow down, look closer, and fall in love with its unique blend of the ancient and the modern.
Sunsets on Busan's Beaches

“Let the golden sands of Busan warm your feet as you stroll along the coastline, savoring fresh seafood and soaking in the spirited rhythms of Korea’s beloved seaside city.”

  • Walk along Haeundae Beach and watch stunning sunsets over the water.
  • Explore the colorful streets of Gamcheon Culture Village.
  • Sample the freshest seafood at Jagalchi Fish Market.
  • Take the Sky Capsule at Blueline Park for coastal views.
  • Discover Busan’s vibrant café culture in Gwangan and Songjeong neighborhoods.
  • Experience lively night markets illuminated with neon lights and ocean breezes.
Jeju Island Adventure

“Escape to Jeju’s wild beauty — where volcanic landscapes meet emerald seas, waterfalls tumble into lush forests, and every breeze carries a scent of adventure.”

  • Hike to the summit of Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak).
  • Visit Jeongbang Waterfall, flowing directly into the ocean.
  • Explore ancient lava tubes like Manjanggul Cave.
  • Relax at Hyeopjae Beach with its turquoise waters and white sands.
  • Wander through green tea fields at O’Sulloc Tea Museum.
  • Meet Jeju’s iconic Dol hareubang stone grandfathers guarding the island.
Autumn Romance at Nami Island

“Wander through dreamy tree-lined paths ablaze with autumn colours at Nami Island, a magical escape where every golden leaf tells a love story.”

  • Walk along tree-lined paths glowing with golden and red foliage in autumn.
  • Rent a bicycle and cycle around the island for scenic views.
  • Pose at romantic landmarks from the famous "Winter Sonata" Korean drama.
  • Enjoy riverside cafés and cozy bakeries along the Han River.
  • Take a short ferry ride to the island or enjoy the scenic zip wire across the river!
