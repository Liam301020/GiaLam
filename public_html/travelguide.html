Travel Guide

Explore the best of Korea with our Travel Guide — from must-do activities and cultural highlights to breathtaking seasonal adventures.

Explore Korea with Us!

Things to Do
Bukhangang Rail Bike

Enjoy a scenic rail bike ride along the beautiful Bukhangang River!

  • Pedal along the Bukhangang River's scenic railway tracks.
  • Admire stunning mountain and river views throughout the journey.
  • Stop for beautiful photo opportunities along the route.
  • Relax at cozy cafés and rest spots near the park.
  • Enjoy a refreshing outdoor activity perfect for families and groups.

  • 📍 Location: 1383, Gimyujeong-ro, Chuncheon-si, Gangwon-do
  • 🕔 Operating Hours:
    • March – November: 9:00, 10:00, 11:00, 12:00, 13:30, 14:30, 15:30, 16:30, 17:30
    • December – February: Same as above except last departure is at 16:30
  • 💵 Pricing (approximate):
    • 2-seater Rail Bike: ₩35,000 (~41 AUD) per bike
    • 4-seater Rail Bike: ₩48,000 (~57 AUD) per bike
    • 4-seater bikes depart first, followed by 2-seaters
  • Advance reservation is recommended, especially on weekends!
KPOP Experience

Attend KPOP concerts, visit entertainment agencies, and learn KPOP dance moves!

  • Visit HYBE Insight and SM Town museums to explore KPOP history and memorabilia.
  • Attend live KPOP concerts or music shows like Inkigayo and Music Bank.
  • Join KPOP dance classes and learn signature choreography moves.
  • Shop for albums, lightsticks, and official KPOP merchandise.

  • 📍 Main Areas: Gangnam, Hongdae, Mapo-gu in Seoul
  • 🕔 Recommended Time: Check event schedules; weekends have more live performances.
  • 💵 Estimated Costs:
    • Dance Class: ~40–60 AUD per session
    • Concert Tickets: starting from ~100 AUD
    • Souvenirs and Merchandise: ~30–80 AUD
  • Tip: Book concert tickets early, they sell out quickly!
Shopping in Myeongdong

Shop the latest fashion, skincare, and KPOP goods at Korea’s trendiest streets!

  • Explore hundreds of cosmetic shops offering top Korean skincare brands.
  • Shop for trendy clothing, accessories, and KPOP-related items.
  • Sample street food like Tteokbokki, Hotteok, and Korean Fried Chicken.
  • Enjoy tax-free shopping for tourists at designated stores.

  • 📍 Location: Myeongdong Shopping Street, Jung-gu, Seoul
  • 🕔 Best Time to Visit: Evening hours (after 5PM) for lively atmosphere and full street food stalls.
  • 💵 Average Spending:
    • Skincare sets: ~30–70 AUD
    • Street food meals: ~5–10 AUD
    • Clothing and accessories: depends on style (~30–100 AUD)
  • Tip: Bring cash (KRW) for small vendors and food stalls!
Food & Culture
Korean Food Experience

Discover the delicious world of Korean cuisine from spicy kimchi to sizzling BBQ!

  • Try authentic dishes like Bibimbap, Kimchi, and Korean Fried Chicken.
  • Visit traditional markets such as Gwangjang and Namdaemun.
  • Join a Korean cooking class experience!
  • 📍 Location: Gwangjang Market, Seoul
  • 🕔 Best Time: Lunch and evening for street food tours
  • 💵 Estimated Costs: Meals ~10–25 AUD per person
Hanbok Experience

Dress up in beautiful Hanbok and stroll through Korea's historic palaces!

  • Rent traditional Hanbok attire for photoshoots at royal palaces.
  • Learn the history and etiquette of Hanbok clothing.
  • 📍 Location: Gyeongbokgung Palace, Seoul
  • 🕔 Best Time: Spring and Autumn for beautiful backgrounds
  • 💵 Estimated Costs: Hanbok rental ~20–30 AUD per 2 hours
Traditional Tea Ceremony

Experience Korea’s elegant tea culture with traditional ceremonies.

  • Participate in a traditional Korean tea ceremony session.
  • Learn the meanings behind Korean tea etiquette and practices.
  • Taste various herbal and flower teas.
  • 📍 Location: Bukchon Hanok Village, Seoul
  • 🕔 Best Time: Morning and afternoon
  • 💵 Estimated Costs: ~25–40 AUD per session
Korean Traditional Festivals

Join vibrant Korean festivals celebrating culture, music, and traditions!

  • Celebrate Chuseok (Korean Thanksgiving) with special foods and games.
  • Join Lunar New Year (Seollal) festivities in palaces and villages.
  • Experience Lotus Lantern Festival with beautiful night parades.
  • 📍 Main Locations: Seoul, Busan, Gyeongju
  • 🕔 Best Time: Chuseok (September), Seollal (January–February), Spring Lantern Festival
  • 💵 Estimated Costs: Mostly free to attend; optional tour packages ~30–50 AUD
Seasonal and Travel Trips
Spring Blossoms in Korea

Experience breathtaking cherry blossoms and vibrant festivals during Korean spring!

  • Visit Yeouido Cherry Blossom Festival in Seoul.
  • Explore Jinhae Cherry Blossom Festival.
  • Perfect season for Hanbok photoshoots!
Summer Adventures

Enjoy Korea’s beaches, water festivals, and vibrant nightlife during summer!

  • Beach trip to Busan or Jeju Island.
  • Attend Boryeong Mud Festival!
  • Night markets and summer fireworks.
Autumn Colors and Festivals

Witness Korea’s breathtaking autumn foliage and cultural celebrations!

  • Walk Nami Island’s golden tree lanes.
  • Explore Seoraksan National Park for peak foliage.
  • Attend Andong Mask Dance Festival.
Winter Wonderland

Enjoy Korea’s snowy landscapes, ski resorts, and festive light festivals!

  • Go skiing at Yongpyong or Vivaldi Park.
  • See the Garden of Morning Calm Winter Lights.
  • Enjoy traditional tteokguk on Lunar New Year!
