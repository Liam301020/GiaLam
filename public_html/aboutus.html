Our History

Journey through our milestones and achievements.

Founded in 2010

Liam’s Visit Korea began with a simple yet powerful vision: to share the vibrant culture, deep-rooted history, and stunning beauty of South Korea with the world. What started as a small, passionate local guide service in the heart of Seoul quickly gained momentum through word-of-mouth recommendations and the heartfelt trust of our early travelers. Our journey began with modest walking tours around the ancient palaces and bustling markets of the capital, but our dreams reached far beyond the city streets.

Growth and Expansion

Over the next decade, fueled by passion and inspired by our guests’ stories, we expanded our horizons beyond Seoul. We crafted tours that ventured into Korea’s hidden gems — tranquil temples tucked away in misty mountains, picturesque coastal towns untouched by time, and vibrant traditional villages echoing with centuries of heritage. Our team of local experts grew, each member bringing specialized knowledge, personal insights, and an unwavering love for Korean culture. Every tour we designed focused not only on sightseeing but on creating meaningful, personalized connections between our guests and Korea’s living traditions.

Today

Today, Liam’s Visit Korea stands proudly as one of the most trusted and beloved tour companies in South Korea. We have been honored to guide thousands of travelers, from solo adventurers to families and corporate groups, each seeking authentic, unforgettable journeys. Our commitment to excellence continues to drive us: curating unique itineraries, preserving cultural authenticity, championing sustainable tourism, and ensuring every guest feels welcomed like family. At Liam’s Visit Korea, every journey we craft is more than a tour — it is an invitation to experience Korea with heart, soul, and wonder.
